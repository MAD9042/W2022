# Assignment A3

# The AR post

Create another post on your blog. This post should be a tutorial on how to build one of the AR apps we've seen in class. If you don't plan to change anything on the apps (or if you don't have an Apple device you can test your changes and updates) you'll have to write TWO posts fot BOTH apps.

Guidelines:

  • Your assignment submission consists of a link to your blog post(s)

  • The reference code for both apps is available to you. Use the links below.

  • The blog post(s) should be a tutorial to help people new to AR to build their own apps

  • Assume your reader know about Xcode and has some experience in iOS development but very little in AR

  • Use videos, screenshots, gists (opens new window) and whatever media could add your tutorial

  • If you are writing just one blog post, you are required to make modifications on the app you choose. Here are some ideas:

    • Combine those two apps into a new one
    • Recreate one of them in SwiftUI
    • Add some 3D Elements to it
    • Create a game using face recognition
    • Use the image recognition tracker to track products in the supermarket
    • Use a different tracker
    • Implement your very own (small*) IDEA!
    • Don't go overboard on this. I want to see something like the Anyone can code (opens new window) tutorials. Simple, complete, to the point.

How to answer and submit this assignment

Open Brighspace and submit the link to your blog.

Reference code

  • Tracker0 - Image (opens new window)
  • Tracke1 - Face (opens new window)

Important

  • There are no limits on the size of the post.
  • DO NOT submit the text for your post, just the link to you blog.
  • Make it look good and professional. Remember, these are PUBLIC posts.
