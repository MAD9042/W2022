# Week 5 - Special Lecture - Marcelo Cabral

Dates

11 February 2022

# Speaker: Marcelo Cabral

Marcelo Cabral is a Software Architect, currently working as Roku Platform team lead for ViacomCBS Streaming (Paramount, CBS, MTV, Nickelodeon among other brands) coming from a 14 years career on Komatsu group, managing teams and developing Embedded, High Precision GPS and IoT solutions for the Mining Industry. He worked on multiple technologies and platforms over the years and once upon time, was an entrepreneur, he also has a bachelor's degree in Business Administration, but can't move away from the fact his passion is being a developer.
